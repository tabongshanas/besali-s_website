1.2 Besali Brief History from 1922 till present date

Besali is the boundary with the following village:

South; Sabes Agong, Mbanga pon-go

North; Mock bin, Fossimondia, Mockleleh

West; Folepi, Bechti, Bamumbu

East; Bangang, Nkong, Alou Sub Division

The people of Besali part are from Fimebai and Fossomondia. The

Village was recognized and signed by the Minister of Territorial

Administration regarded to the reference No: 1559/9799/1922.

During this period of the 1923 Njockneji rule as the first Fon of

Besali and Taku Tanyi serve as Nkwete. The two brothers Fon and

Nkwete are from Fimebai. When the Eponymous Njockneiji

disappear, Taku Tanyi was instantaneously enthroned. Forbai is

the son of Tanjoh Acha Forkwai in that order. The next fon of

Besali was Tanjoh Acha. And Atiaboh followed by Tanjoh Tayem.

Fotabong help Fon Tanjoh Acha to capture the Abasap- Besali

people from

Fossimondia. Forjue did all best of go-between among the Fons.

So Forbai, Forkwai, Forjue, and Fotabong are the chiefs who

fought for the gorgeousness of this village and the 'German war'.

The village is making members of Chiefs and the Bekems/Nkem.

1.3 Traditional Way of Life

As the Fon, he has the right to enter any meetinghouse. He is

personal in those the traditions affirm him to belong. We cannot

call him Fon, Chief but use the traditional word “Fouw”.
